About ERA RAM

The Ethiopian Road Administration (ERA) Road Asset Management (RAM) is responsible for overseeing and optimizing the maintenance and budget allocation for roads and bridges across the country. Managing 11 districts, ERA RAM ensures that infrastructure remains safe and efficient by tracking the condition of roads and bridges, analyzing data, and adjusting budgets as necessary. This systematic approach helps to prioritize critical repairs, upgrades, and resource allocation to meet both short-term and long-term goals. By closely monitoring these assets, ERA RAM supports Ethiopia’s infrastructure development, ensuring that the country’s road network continues to serve the growing needs of its population and economy.
